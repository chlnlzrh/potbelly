## **What Makes Phase 1 Work for Pooja:**

### **1. Everything is Visible**

- Not hiding anything
- Can see all 35+ tasks if she wants
- But default view shows what matters most

### **2. Simple to Navigate**

- Just 3 tabs (Home, Tasks, Team)
- Swipe left/right
- No complex menus

### **3. One-Tap Actions**

- See task → Tap to expand → Tap to call
- Never more than 2 taps to take action

### **4. Multiple Ways to Find Things**
